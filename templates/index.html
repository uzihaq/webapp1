Quiz and Answer Key Generator

This app will create a quiz and an answer key based on the data you provide.

  1. Step 1: Upload an answer key in CSV format. Make sure there are only 2 columns, the first with the question, and the second with the answer.
  2. Step 2: How many questions do you want your quiz to have? It will take a random selection of questions from your answer sheet for each quiz.
  3. Step 3: How many different quizzes do you want?
  4. Step 4: Download your quizzes in a zip file!
